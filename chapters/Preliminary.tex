% AGRADECER!

The work here presented is delivered as final thesis report at Instituto Superior Técnico (IST) in Lisbon, Portugal and it is in partial fulfillment of the European Master in Distributed Computing belonging to cohort of 2012-2014. The joint Erasmus Mundus Master Programme has been composed of a first year at IST, a second year’s first semester at Royal Institute of Technology (KTH), and for this work and last academic term, a degree project at Computer Systems Laboratory in Swedish Institute of Computer Science - SICS (Stockholm, Sweden).

\noindent Special thanks to my advisor Dr. Jim Dowling for his fully support throughout the project. Jim is a cool guy and he will always be patient to help. \noindent I am thankful to my family who always support any of my life decisions, especially my father.
